Sonic Era

Sonic was a blue hedgehog who lived in the southern islands(green hill) until one day an evil scientist called Dr.Ivo Robotnik, he wanted to dominate the world and to do so, he kidnapped animals using them as a source of energy to his robots (badnicks), so Sonic chases him and gathers the chaos emeralds to defeat him in his super form, Sonic defeats him and destroys the Death Egg, but Eggman manages to escape.


Some time later, Sonic meets Miles Tails Prower, an extremely intelligent, two-tailed yellow fox who would become his best friend, as well as helping to defeat Eggman again who returned in search of world domination.
